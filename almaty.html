The 28 Panfilov Guardsmen Park

The Park named after 28 Panfilov Guards is one of the most famous and beloved parks in Almaty, Kazakhstan. It was founded in 1947 and named in honor of 28 heroically killed Guards, including Panfilov, during the defense of Moscow during the Great Patriotic War. The park is located in the historical center of the city, next to Panfilov Square and Zenkov Cathedral. It is famous for its beautiful alley with tall and shady trees, a pond with swans and ducks, as well as many monuments and sculptures. In the park you can spend time relaxing and walking, visit the playground, enjoy nature and architecture. It is also a popular venue for celebrations, events and concerts.

The Ascension Cathedral

The Ascension Cathedral, also known as Zenkovsky Cathedral, is an Orthodox church in Almaty, Kazakhstan. Built in the early 20th century (1904-1907), it is one of the oldest and most famous architectural landmarks of the city. The cathedral was designed by architect Andrey Pavlovich Zenkov and is unique in that it is completely made of wood without the use of nails, which makes it one of the tallest wooden buildings in the world. The cathedral building stands out for its bright architecture and colorful exterior decoration. Inside, the cathedral is decorated with beautiful icons and frescoes, which makes it an important religious and cultural center for Orthodox believers. The Ascension Cathedral also survived an earthquake in 1911, which testifies to its strength and craftsmanship of construction. Today, the Ascension Cathedral is not only an active temple, but also a popular tourist attraction. It is located in the park named after 28 Panfilov Guards, which makes it easily accessible to visitors. The cathedral plays an important role in the spiritual life of the city and is a symbol of the historical and cultural heritage of Almaty.

Kok Tobe

Kok Tobe is a mountain and a popular tourist complex in Almaty, Kazakhstan. Located at an altitude of 1100 meters, it offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city and the picturesque mountains. The summit can be reached by funicular or by a winding road. The Kok Tobe complex includes many attractions and entertainment. There is an amusement park with attractions for children and adults, a small zoo, observation decks with stunning views, restaurants with a variety of cuisine, as well as the famous statue of The Beatles, which attracts many tourists. In addition, Kok Tobe is a venue for various cultural and entertainment events, which makes it one of the most visited and favorite places for both locals and tourists.
